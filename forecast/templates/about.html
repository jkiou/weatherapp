About Weather App

Welcome to our Weather Application

This weather application provides real-time weather information and forecasts for cities worldwide. Using data from OpenWeatherMap, we deliver accurate and up-to-date weather information including:

  • Current temperature and conditions
  • Humidity and cloud coverage
  • Wind speed and pressure
  • 5-day weather forecast
  • Beautiful weather-based backgrounds
